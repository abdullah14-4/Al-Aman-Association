Exchange Visit Between Al-Halali School Students and Educational Construction School

In coordination with the School of the Martyr Fadl Al-Halali, the Department of Integration at AOBWC implemented today,

Saturday 5/3/2022, the exchange visit between the fifth-grade students and the Educational Construction Schools. The

visit aimed to promote the concept of integration and break the barrier of fear among the students who are about to move

to integration schools. They were also introduced to the systems and methods followed in the integration schools.


The blind students were received by Ms Jihan, the director of the Educational Construction School and the school’s

students. They attended for a full school day with their fellow sighted students. And at the end of the day, the blind

students expressed their thanks to the school’s management, staff and students for their hospitality, their warm welcome.
