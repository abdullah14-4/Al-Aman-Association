Course on the English Language in Braille

AOBWC implemented a course on the English language in Braille, that targeted 24 female students in primary and

secondary education. The course aimed to improve the students in the subject and to teach them reading and writing

English in Braille. The course included teaching 5 students reading and writing Arabic in Braille.
