AOBWC Received an Inspection Visit from SCMCHA and the Fund for the Care and Rehabilitation of the Disabled


AOBWC, represented by Ms. Sabah Huraish, Head of the Organization, and Ms. Saba Quhaim, General Manager of the

Organization, received today, Tuesday, March 1, 2022, Mr. Jaber Al-Razhi, Director of the branch of the Supreme Council

for the Management and Coordination of Humanitarian Affairs and International Cooperation (SCMCHA) in Hodeidah, and

Dr. Ali Nasser Moghli, Executive Director of the Fund of the Rehabilitation and Care of People with Disabilities, on an

inspection visit to the Organization. A tripartite meeting was held during which issues of the disabled and the difficulties

they face were discussed. The three parties also discussed ways of cooperation between all parties to face these difficulties.

At the end of the meeting, Ms. Sabah handed over shields of thanks and gratitude to the two parties for their continuous

support to the projects and activities of the Organization.
