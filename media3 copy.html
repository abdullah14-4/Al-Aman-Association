Concluding the Rehabilitation Program “Intelaka”

On Wednesday, June 22, 2022, AOBWC, in cooperation with Bena Foundation for Training and Consultation, concluded the

rehabilitation program “Intelaka (A Start) Programme”, which targeted girls from 13 to 16 years old, in which 10 blind

integrated students participated

The program started on June 4 and lasted for three weeks. It included many training courses and community initiatives.

The blind students were keen to get the full benefit of this program to help them actively participate in society.
