Workshop of Midterm Review of Education Centers


AOBWC implemented on Tuesday, December 21, 2021, a workshop entitled (Midterm Review of Education Centers ). The

workshop aimed to discuss the implementation of the strategic plan in the educational facilities during the first term. It

was produced by Mr. Mukhtar Al Hammadi, Education Quality Consultant. And it was attended by 28 leaders of the

Organization and its educational facilities and teachers of the facilities.
