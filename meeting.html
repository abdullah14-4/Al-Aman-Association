First Consultation Meeting on the Funding Allocated to the Disabled

Coordinated by AOBWC and sponsored by the Fund for the Care and Rehabilitation of the Disabled, the first Consultation meeting was held today, Tuesday, March 22, 2022, to coordinate efforts of government authorities

people with disabilities on the funding allocated to the disabled from donors. The meeting discussed the most important ssues related to people with disabilities and how to find solutions to them in cooperation With government authorities

and authorities concerned. The attendees were received by Ms Sabah Huraish (the Organization Head) and Ms Saba Quhaim (the Organization General Manager).



this meeting was attended by:

  • Dr. Ali Nasser Moghli – Executive Director of the Fund for the Disabled
  • Mr. Abdullah Bunyan – Chairman of the National Union of Yemeni Disabled Associations
  • Mr. Abd al-Salam al-Nawab – Director General of Local Organizations and Partners in the General Secretariat of the Supreme Council (SCMCHA)
  • Mr. Abdullah Abu Talib – Director General of Displacement and Emergencies at the Supreme Council
  • Mr. Ahmed Al-Asoudi – General Director of Social Coordination in the Capital Municipality
  • Mr. Rasheed Mofaddal – General Manager of the Supreme Council Branch (SCMCHA) in the Secretariat
  • Mr. Muhammad Abu Areej – General Director of Centers and Houses in the Capital Municipality
