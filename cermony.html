Ceremony on the Occasion of the 23rd Anniversary of the AOBWC Establishment

AOBWC held on Thursday, June 9, 2022, a ceremony on the occasion of the 23rd anniversary of the Organization’s

establishment, in the presence of the Organization’s staff and members of its general assembly.

The event included different parts and a word delivered by Ms. Sabah Huraish, the Head of the Organization, in which she

congratulated the staff and all attendees on the accomplishments achieved by the Organization until this moment,

praising the role of all the employees.

Two success stories of blind women who work in government and private agencies outside the Organization were also

presented. And on the sidelines of the event, the annual general assembly meeting was held to review and unanimously

approve the 2021 report.
