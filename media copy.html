Election of the Administrative Body and the Monitoring & Inspections Committee / the Sixth Session


On Wednesday, July 27, 2022, AOBWC held an event for electing the administrative Body and the Monitoring & Inspections Committee for the sixth session

The event was attended by Mr. Abdullah Bunyan, head of the National Union of Yemeni Disabled Associations, with a number of officials, and 77 of the 89 members of the General Assembly.

At the beginning of the event, the Organization’s administrative and financial reports were approved by the members of the General Assembly

After the resignation of the previous Administrative Body and the Monitoring & Inspections Committee, the nomination and voting processes took place in a democratic and transparent manner, resulting in a new Administrative Body and Monitoring & Inspections Committee.
