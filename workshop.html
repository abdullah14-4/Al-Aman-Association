Launching Anamil Sewing Workshop


AOBWC Organization, represented by Ms. Sabah Huraish, Head of the Organization, and Ms. Saba Quhaim, General

Director of the Organization, launched today, Thursday, December 23, 2021, Anamil Sewing Workshop, in the presence of

Dr. Ali Moghli, Executive Director of the Fund for the Care and Rehabilitation of the Disabled, and Hassan Al-Masoari,

Deputy Director of the Office of Social Affairs and Labor. Anamil workshop is a project of rehabilitating the families of blind

women and training them in sewing with the aim of empowering them economically.

Some of the trainees’ products were exposed at the event, and all the attendees expressed their admiration for the high

quality of the products. They also expressed their happiness with this project and emphasized its importance in helping

blind women and their families and alleviating the difficult economic conditions that the country suffers from
